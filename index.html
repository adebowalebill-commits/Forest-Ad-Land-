What is Forest Ad Land?

Forest Ad Land is a next-generation digital world where virtual land, advertising, and community come together. It's designed to create value through ownership, visibility, and long-term growth. It gives users, creators, and businesses a shared space to own digital land, showcase brands, and become part of a growing community built for the future.
