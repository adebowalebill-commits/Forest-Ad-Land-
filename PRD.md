# Forest Ad Land - Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## 1. Project Vision & Goals
- **Vision:** To create a global digital land and advertising ecosystem where users, businesses, and a utility token interact seamlessly. This is not just a token or a meme, but a sustainable product with a long-term mindset.
- **Core Goals:** 
  - Build a working environment where land ownership is *active* rather than passive.
  - Expand eventually across different digital cities.
  - Prove real utility and stable liquidity before pursuing major exchange listings.

## 2. Target Audience
- **Regular Users & Early Supporters:** Community members looking for digital property ownership, marketplace trading, and reward opportunities. Early supporters are highly valued and will receive special roles, priority features, and starting opportunities.
- **Businesses & Brands:** Local businesses, creators, tourism projects, hotels, and real estate-related brands looking for innovative visual advertising formats.

## 3. Core Product Mechanics

### A. Digital Property Types
- **Regular Land Plots:** Standard digital real estate.
- **Premium Locations & City Zones:** High-traffic areas with potentially higher earning potential.
- **Billboard / Ad Spaces:** Dedicated spots optimized for business advertising.
- **Business & Community Areas:** Specialized zones for specific interactions.

### B. Acquisition & Marketplace
- **Initial Distribution:** Early community access, rewards for active users, and limited early plots. Avoid a strictly "pay to enter" model initially.
- **Marketplace:** A full ecosystem for users to buy, trade, and sell digital land and advertising spaces.

### C. Active Ownership & Utility
- Ownership must unlock utility: advertising placement, visibility, property upgrades, ecosystem rewards, and interactions with businesses.

### D. Advertising Engine
- A system that allows businesses to easily purchase and place visual ads on digital properties, driving revenue to land owners and the ecosystem.

### E. Token Utility Layer
- The token is the connection point of the ecosystem, used for: land-related actions, advertising, upgrades, rewards, and marketplace activity.
- **Multi-Payment Approach:** To ensure the product remains easy for regular users and businesses, multiple payment options (including fiat/traditional) will be supported alongside the token.

## 4. Platform Strategy
- **Phase 1 (MVP):** Web Application. Chosen for faster launch, ease of testing, and cross-device availability without app store friction.
- **Phase 2:** Mobile Application development once the core product is validated and stable.

## 5. Go-To-Market & Milestones
- **Public Roadmap:** Maintain a transparent, public roadmap showing what is built, tested, and coming next. No fake hype.
- **Business Onboarding:** Start with small test campaigns to demonstrate real value to early business partners.
- **Listing Strategy:** No rush to list. Listings will only happen after achieving a working product, growing user base, stable liquidity, and proof of organic engagement.

## 6. Key Challenges to Overcome
- Building trust and avoiding "short-term hype" culture.
- Balancing the token economy.
- Growing a real, organic community and proving genuine use-cases.
