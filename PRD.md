# Product Requirements Document (PRD) — Forest Ad Land

## 1. Project Overview

**Forest Ad Land** is a Web3 ecosystem that connects digital land ownership, advertising spaces, businesses, users, and a utility token into a single product. It is **not** a meme coin or short-term hype project — it is a long-term vision to build a global digital land and advertising platform.

The landing page serves as the **public-facing introduction** to the project. Its sole purpose is to clearly communicate what Forest Ad Land is, what it aims to build, and how people can get involved early.

---

## 2. Core Concepts

### The Token
- Acts as the **utility layer** inside the ecosystem.
- Used for: land-related actions, advertising, upgrades, rewards, marketplace activity, and community incentives.
- Not the entire product — it is a **connection point** within a larger ecosystem.
- Multiple payment options may exist in the future, but the token remains a key pillar.

### Digital Land & Property
- Users can own, trade, and use digital land plots within a virtual map/city.
- **Property types** (planned):
  - Regular land plots
  - Premium locations
  - Billboard / ad spaces
  - City zones
  - Business areas
  - Special community zones
- Each type has different utility and earning potential based on demand, traffic, and location.
- **Ownership is active, not passive** — land can generate value through advertising placement, visibility, upgrades, rewards, marketplace activity, and future city expansion.

### Advertising Ecosystem
- Businesses can purchase or rent advertising space on digital land.
- Creates real demand from outside the crypto community (businesses, creators, brands).
- Connects Web3 land ownership with real-world advertising utility.

### Marketplace
- Users can buy, sell, and trade digital land and ad spaces.
- Creates organic economic activity between landowners and businesses.

## 4. Landing Page Purpose

The landing page is **informational only**. It should:

1. **Explain** what Forest Ad Land is in simple, clear terms.
2. **Communicate** the long-term vision (not hype).
3. **Show** the public roadmap and current progress.
4. **Present** the token utility and contract address.
5. **Link** to community channels (Telegram, X/Twitter).
6. **Build trust** — transparency, no fake promises, real progress.

The page is NOT:
- A dApp or interactive product.
- A token sale / ICO page.
- A marketplace or dashboard.

---

## 5. Key Content Sections (Landing Page)

1. **Hero Section** — Bold introduction to Forest Ad Land. Tagline + CTA to join community.
2. **About / Vision** — What the project is and what it aims to become.
3. **How It Works** — Simple explanation of the ecosystem (land, ads, token, marketplace).
4. **Token Utility** — What the token does, contract address (CA).
5. **Property Types** — Overview of digital land categories and their value.
6. **Roadmap** — Public, transparent, step-by-step progress tracker.
7. **Early Community Benefits** — Why getting in early matters.
8. **Community / Socials** — Links to Telegram, X, email.
9. **Footer** — CA, disclaimers, links.

---

## 6. Public Roadmap Philosophy

- Community should **clearly see** what stage the project is in.
- Show what has been built, what's being tested, and what comes next.
- Built **openly and honestly** — no fake hype or unrealistic promises.
- Phases should reflect real milestones, not marketing fluff.

---

## 7. Early Community & Acquisition Strategy

- **Not** a "pay to enter" system exclusively.
- Early mechanisms include:
  - Early community access
  - Rewards for active users
  - Limited early plots
  - Later: full marketplace for buying/trading
- Early supporters get: early access, special roles, priority features, limited community rewards, better starting opportunities.

---

## 8. Long-Term Vision

- Expand beyond one map/city into **multiple cities globally**.
- Become a **global digital land and advertising ecosystem**.
- Economy driven by **real usage**, not speculation.
- Biggest growth catalyst: when people see this is a real product with long-term value for businesses, creators, and everyday users.

### Exchange/Listing Philosophy
- No rush to list on major exchanges.
- Prerequisites: working product, growing community, active users, clear token utility, stable liquidity, organic engagement, proof of substance.
